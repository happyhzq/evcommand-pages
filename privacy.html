EV Command Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

EV Command helps electric vehicle owners monitor vehicle status, charging, battery health, trips, reminders and owner workflows. We design the app to keep personal data limited and transparent.

Information We Collect

Account information you enter, such as name, email and password, is currently stored locally on your device in this version. Vehicle, charging, trip and battery health screens use demo or user-entered data unless you explicitly connect a supported vehicle data source in a future release. Purchase status is handled through Apple In-App Purchase.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features, remember your selected language, show membership status, support account creation, sign-in, sign-out and account deletion flows, and improve reliability. We do not sell personal information.

Vehicle Data

EV Command is not affiliated with Tesla, Inc. The app does not require a Tesla account for the demo experience. If a future version adds vehicle API connection, the app will request authorization before accessing vehicle data and will describe the data used at that time.

Purchases

EV Command Pro subscriptions and lifetime unlocks are processed by Apple. Apple may provide us with transaction identifiers and entitlement status so the app can unlock paid features.

Account Deletion

You can delete the local EV Command account from the Account screen in the app. Deletion removes the locally stored account and membership display state from the app. If cloud account features are introduced later, this policy will be updated with the cloud deletion process.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support through the EV Command support page.
