Common Topics

Account: create, sign in, sign out and delete your local account from the Account tab.

Language: choose English, Simplified Chinese, Cantonese, Korean, Japanese or Spanish from Settings.

Purchases: use the Membership screen to buy or restore EV Command Pro through Apple In-App Purchase.

Vehicle data: the current review build uses demo vehicle data and does not require a live vehicle login.
