EV Command Terms of Use

Last updated: July 14, 2026

EV Command provides optional read-only vehicle monitoring, clearly labeled demo information and owner tools for informational use. EV Command is not affiliated with Tesla, Inc. or any vehicle manufacturer.

Use of the App

Do not rely on EV Command as the sole source for driving, charging, safety, service or emergency decisions. Always follow your vehicle's official displays, manuals and service guidance.

Accounts and Vehicle Connection

An EV Command account uses Sign in with Apple. Connecting Tesla is optional and requires your consent on Tesla's authorization screen. The current release requests read-only data access and does not send vehicle commands. You are responsible for protecting access to your device and accounts.

Data Accuracy and Availability

Live and cached states are labeled in the app. Vehicle data may be delayed, unavailable or incomplete because of vehicle sleep, network conditions, regional API availability or third-party services. Demo values are sample fixtures and do not describe your vehicle.

Paid Features

EV Command Pro Lifetime is a one-time non-consumable purchase through Apple In-App Purchase and does not auto-renew. Apple manages payment, restoration and refund eligibility.

Account Deletion

You can delete your EV Command cloud account from the Account screen. Deletion removes EV Command account and connected-vehicle records, but does not cancel or delete purchases managed by Apple.
