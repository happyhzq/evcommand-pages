EV Command

A multilingual command center for EV owners: live-style vehicle status, charging insights, battery health, trips, reminders, service records, account controls and App Store membership.

  • Monitor range, energy, odometer, cycles and battery health.
  • Track charging behavior, costs, stations and off-peak plans.
  • Manage account creation, sign-in, deletion and paid membership.
  • Switch between English, Simplified Chinese, Cantonese, Korean, Japanese and Spanish.
